# 🌸 ARTAVA - Project Vision

## Project Name

ARTAVA

---
## LILAC


## Tagline

Know Your Cycle. Understand Your Body.

---

## Vision

ARTAVA is an AI-powered menstrual health companion that helps users track, understand, and analyze their menstrual cycles through intelligent insights, personalized predictions, and health analytics.

The goal is to empower users with accurate information, privacy-first health tracking, and meaningful trends—not just date predictions.

---

## Mission

To create a modern, intelligent, and user-friendly menstrual wellness platform that makes menstrual health easier to understand and manage.

---

## Core Values

- Privacy First
- Scientific Accuracy
- AI-Assisted Insights
- Simplicity
- Accessibility
- Inclusivity

---

## Target Audience

Primary:
- College students
- Working professionals

Future:
- Teenagers
- Women with PCOS
- Pregnancy planning users
- Healthcare professionals

---

## Long-Term Goal

Become one of the most intelligent menstrual health platforms using AI, data visualization, and predictive analytics.
